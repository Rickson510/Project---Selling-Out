"DISCOVER THE NIKE STORY"

Our Vision

At Nike, we believe in the power of sport to transform lives. Our mission is to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete in the world, and we define athlete as anyone with a body. From professional athletes to weekend warriors, from kids to seniors, we are here to help people reach their full potential and unleash their inner athlete.

We are driven by our passion for creating products that help people reach their full potential. From our iconic sneakers to our high-performance apparel, we strive to design and manufacture the best products in the industry. Our innovative technologies, such as Nike Flyknit, Nike React, and Nike Air, are constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

Our Approch

Today, Nike is a global leader in sports apparel, footwear, and equipment, with a presence in more than 190 countries. We offer a wide range of products for all types of sports, including running, basketball, football, soccer, and golf, as well as lifestyle products for everyday wear. Our products are designed with cutting-edge technology and innovation, and are tested and trusted by some of the world's best athletes.

But Nike is more than just a sports brand. We are committed to making a positive impact on the world, and we believe that sport can be a force for good. We invest in programs that promote physical activity and healthy lifestyles, and we work to create sustainable products and reduce our environmental impact. We also support athletes who use their platforms to drive social change and advance important causes.

Our Process

At Nike, we are proud of our heritage and our commitment to innovation, diversity, and inclusion. We believe that everyone has the potential to be an athlete, and we are here to help you unleash your full potential. Whether you're a seasoned pro or just getting started, we invite you to join us on this journey and be part of the Nike community.

We are also dedicated to fostering a culture of diversity and inclusion, where everyone is welcome and valued. We believe that different perspectives and experiences make us stronger and more innovative. That's why we work to create a workplace and a community where everyone can thrive, regardless of their background.
